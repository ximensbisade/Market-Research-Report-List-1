<p><strong>The Orthopedic Fixation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixation Device</li><li>Internal Fixation Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic fixation device market is categorized into two primary types: external fixation devices and internal fixation devices. External fixation devices are applied outside the body to stabilize fractures or support limb deformities, using pins and rods. In contrast, internal fixation devices involve the implantation of hardware such as plates, screws, or nails inside the body to align and secure fractured bones during the healing process. Both types play crucial roles in orthopedic surgery, aiding recovery and restoring functionality.</p></p>
