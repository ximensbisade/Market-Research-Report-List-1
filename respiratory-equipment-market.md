<p><p>The respiratory equipment market comprises three main types: therapeutic devices, monitoring devices, and diagnostic devices. Therapeutic devices are designed to manage and treat respiratory diseases, such as nebulizers and ventilators. Monitoring devices track respiratory parameters, helping healthcare professionals assess patient conditions in real time, including pulse oximeters and capnometers. Diagnostic devices facilitate the identification of respiratory disorders through tests and imaging, such as spirometers and chest X-ray machines, providing critical information for effective treatment decisions.</p></p>
