<p><p>Distributed storage systems can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems involve local data storage within an organization’s infrastructure, offering control over data security and compliance, but requiring significant capital investment and maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based storage utilizes remote servers managed by third-party providers, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced costs without the need for physical infrastructure. Both types cater to different organizational needs, balancing control and cost-effectiveness in data management.</p></p>
