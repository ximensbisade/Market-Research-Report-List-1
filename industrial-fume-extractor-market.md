<p><p>The industrial fume extractor market consists of two primary types: fixed and mobile. Fixed-type extractors are installed in a specific location, providing permanent solutions for capturing hazardous fumes in environments such as factories and welding shops. They are effective for consistent sources of emissions. In contrast, mobile-type extractors are portable units designed for flexibility, allowing operators to move them to different work areas as needed. Both types are essential for maintaining air quality and ensuring worker safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
