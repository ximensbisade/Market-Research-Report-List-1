<p><p>The ID gripping market encompasses three primary types: electric grippers, pneumatic grippers, and manual grippers. Electric grippers utilize motors for precise control and flexibility, making them ideal for automated processes. Pneumatic grippers rely on compressed air to deliver high-speed operation and adaptability, commonly used in industrial settings. Manual grippers offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness, allowing operators to handle tasks without automation. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying demands for precision, speed, and operational efficiency in material handling.</p></p>
