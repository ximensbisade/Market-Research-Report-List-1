<p><p>The pet identification chip market primarily comprises three types: 125 KHz, 128 KHz, and 134.2 KHz chips. Each frequency corresponds to different standards for animal identification. The 125 KHz chips are commonly used for basic identification, while the 128 KHz chips provide improved data transfer capabilities and compatibility with various readers. The 134.2 KHz chips are increasingly favored globally due to their alignment with ISO standards, ensuring universal readability and better traceability for lost pets.</p></p>
