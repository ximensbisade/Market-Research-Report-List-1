<p><p>The smart luggage tag market features two primary technologies: QR codes and NFC (Near Field Communication). QR code tags allow users to scan the code with a smartphone to access flight details and track their luggage. They are cost-effective and easy to use but require an internet connection. NFC tags, on the other hand, enable contactless communication between devices, allowing users to tap their phone to retrieve information instantly. Both technologies enhance luggage tracking and improve the travel experience.</p></p>
