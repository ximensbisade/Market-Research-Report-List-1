<p><p>Food safety testing systems and services encompass several key methods utilized to ensure food quality and compliance. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a molecular technique for detecting pathogens at a genetic level. Immunoassays use antibodies to identify specific contaminants. Chromatography separates compounds for analysis of additives and toxins. Inductive Coupled Plasma (ICP) analyzes elemental composition for heavy metals and other minerals. Culture media supports the growth of microorganisms for traditional microbiological testing, essential for identifying spoilage and pathogens.</p></p>
