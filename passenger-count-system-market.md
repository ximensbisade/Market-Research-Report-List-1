<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Passenger Count System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Time-of-Flight Type</li><li>Stereoscopic Vision Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passenger Count System market comprises three primary types. The Infrared Type utilizes infrared sensors to detect passengers entering or exiting a space by measuring interrupted beams of light. The Time-of-Flight Type employs laser or LED technology to calculate the distance between the sensor and the passenger, enabling accurate counting based on travel time. Meanwhile, the Stereoscopic Vision Type uses dual cameras to create a 3D image, allowing for precise detection and counting of passengers through advanced image processing techniques.</p></p>
