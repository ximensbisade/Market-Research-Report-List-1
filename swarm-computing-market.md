<p><p>Swarm computing encompasses various optimization techniques inspired by natural phenomena. Ant Colony Optimization mimics the foraging behavior of ants to find optimal paths in complex environments, making it suitable for routing and scheduling challenges. Particle Swarm Optimization, inspired by social behavior in birds, optimizes solutions through collaborative movement of particles in a search space, effectively tackling multidimensional optimization problems.</p></p>
