<p><p>The multiport plug valves market is segmented into three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric valves. Manual multiport plug valves require physical operation for control, offering simplicity and reliability in low-traffic applications. Pneumatic multiport plug valves utilize compressed air for actuation, providing faster and more efficient operation suitable for automated systems. Electric multiport plug valves use electric power for operation, enabling precise control and integration with digital systems, making them ideal for complex and high-demand environments.</p></p>
