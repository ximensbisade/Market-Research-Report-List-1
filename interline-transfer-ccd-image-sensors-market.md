<p><strong>The Interline Transfer CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Frame CCD Image Sensors</li><li>Frame-Transfer CCD Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interline transfer CCD image sensors are critical in capturing high-quality images, utilized in various applications. The full-frame CCD image sensors offer superior image quality by capturing light across the entire sensor area, making them ideal for low-light conditions. In contrast, frame-transfer CCD image sensors store image data in a separate area of the sensor, enabling faster readout times and reducing motion blur, which is particularly beneficial in high-speed imaging scenarios. Both types cater to specific needs in the imaging market.</p></p>
