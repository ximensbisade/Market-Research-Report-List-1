<p><p>Elastomeric alloys (EAs) are advanced materials combining elastomer properties with metallic elements, offering enhanced flexibility and durability. The market features various types based on their metallic compositions. Copper-based alloys provide good conductivity and corrosion resistance, while Fe-Ni-Cr alloys are known for their high-temperature stability. Ni-Ct alloys offer excellent strength and wear resistance, while Ni-Cr-Nb and Ni-Co-Cr formulations enhance fatigue resistance. Nb-Ti alloys improve strength-to-weight ratios, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. These variations cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
