<p><p>The SQF Furnace market comprises two primary types: Straight Through Type and In-Out Type. The Straight Through Type allows materials to flow continuously, facilitating efficient heating and processing in a linear manner, making it ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, the In-Out Type operates with materials entering and exiting at designated points, allowing for more controlled processing and versatility in handling different products. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and productivity in thermal processing operations.</p></p>
