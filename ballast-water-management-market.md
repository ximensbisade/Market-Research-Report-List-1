<p><p>The Ballast Water Management Market encompasses three primary types: physical disinfection, mechanical methods, and chemical methods. Physical disinfection involves techniques like UV radiation and heat treatment to eliminate aquatic organisms. Mechanical methods focus on filtration and separation processes to remove contaminants from ballast water. Chemical methods utilize biocides or eco-friendly chemicals to kill invasive species. Each method aims to prevent the transfer of harmful organisms in ballast water, ensuring compliance with international regulations and protecting marine ecosystems.</p></p>
