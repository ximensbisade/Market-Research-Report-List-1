<p><p>Bipolar electrosurgery is a surgical technique that utilizes electrical currents to cut tissue and control bleeding during procedures. It involves the use of a bipolar electrode, which focuses the electrical energy between two tips, providing precise cutting and coagulation with minimal thermal damage to surrounding tissues. This method is particularly favored in delicate surgeries, such as those involving the brain, spine, and other sensitive areas.</p><p>The Bipolar Electrosurgery Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
