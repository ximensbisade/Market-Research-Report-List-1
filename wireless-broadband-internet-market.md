<p><p>The wireless broadband internet market consists of two primary types: Fixed Wireless Broadband and Mobile Wireless Broadband. Fixed Wireless Broadband provides high-speed internet access to specific locations using fixed antennas, making it ideal for homes and businesses in areas lacking wired options. Conversely, Mobile Wireless Broadband offers internet connectivity through cellular networks, allowing users to access the internet on-the-go via smartphones, tablets, and portable hotspots. Both types cater to diverse user needs but differ in mobility and installation requirements.</p></p>
