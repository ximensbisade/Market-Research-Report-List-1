<p><strong>The PTFE Dispersion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTFE Oil Dispersion</li><li>PTFE Aqueous Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE dispersion market comprises two primary types: PTFE oil dispersion and PTFE aqueous dispersion. PTFE oil dispersion is created by suspending polytetrafluoroethylene in oil, enhancing lubrication and reducing friction in industrial applications. In contrast, PTFE aqueous dispersion involves dispersing PTFE in water, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and other water-based applications. Both types are valued for their unique properties, including chemical resistance and low surface energy, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, electronics, and textiles.</p></p>
