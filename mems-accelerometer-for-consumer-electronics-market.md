<p><p>MEMS accelerometers are pivotal in consumer electronics, enhancing device functionality. </p><p>1-axis MEMS accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, often used in basic applications. </p><p>2-axis models capture movement in two dimensions, ideal for simple orientation detection. </p><p>3-axis accelerometers provide comprehensive motion data across three dimensions, widely used in smartphones and wearables. </p><p>6-axis MEMS integrate accelerometers and gyroscopes for advanced motion tracking, while 9-axis sensors combine accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers, enabling precise navigation and orientation in complex applications.</p></p>
