<p><p>Power quality meters are crucial for monitoring and analyzing the electrical parameters of power systems. The market for these meters is divided into two main types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase power quality meters are typically used for residential and light commercial applications, measuring parameters in a single-phase circuit. In contrast, three-phase power quality meters serve industrial and large commercial applications, monitoring multi-phase systems to ensure stability and efficiency. Each type is tailored to specific power configurations and user needs.</p></p>
