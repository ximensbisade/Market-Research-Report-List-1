<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>M Wave</li><li>Decimeter Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless headset for gaming market can be categorized into three types based on frequency ranges: Infrared, M Wave, and Decimeter Wave. Infrared headsets use light waves for communication, providing a secure connection but limited range. M Wave headsets operate in the microwave frequency range, offering better audio quality and longer distances but may experience interference. Decimeter Wave headsets leverage radio frequencies, suitable for high-fidelity audio and extensive ranges, making them popular among gamers for immersive experiences and flexibility.</p></p>
