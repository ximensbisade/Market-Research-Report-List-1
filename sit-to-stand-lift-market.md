<p><p>The sit-to-stand lift market is divided into two primary types: manual sit-to-stand lifts and electric sit-to-stand lifts. Manual sit-to-stand lifts require caregivers to assist individuals in transitioning from sitting to standing, using mechanical components that rely on human effort. In contrast, electric sit-to-stand lifts feature motorized systems, allowing for powered movement and reducing strain on caregivers. Both types are designed to enhance mobility for individuals with limited strength, offering safety and support during transfers between different positions.</p></p>
