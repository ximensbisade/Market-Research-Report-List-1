<p><p>The paper-based laminates market is primarily divided into two types: Low Pressure Laminates (LPL) and High Pressure Laminates (HPL). LPLs are created by pressing layers of paper with a resin under low pressure, making them suitable for decorative surfaces and functional applications at lower costs. In contrast, HPLs involve high-pressure techniques that produce more durable and resilient materials, ideal for heavy-duty applications like countertops and flooring. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing aesthetic appeal and functionality.</p></p>
