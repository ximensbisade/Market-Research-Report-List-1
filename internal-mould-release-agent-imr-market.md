<p><p>Internal Mould Release Agents (IMR) are specialized formulations used in manufacturing processes, particularly in composite and plastic industries, to facilitate the easy removal of products from molds. The market for IMRs is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based IMR and water-based IMR. Solvent-based IMRs offer rapid evaporation and excellent lubrication but may pose environmental and health risks. In contrast, water-based IMRs are more environmentally friendly, ensuring safer application and reduced VOC emissions, making them increasingly popular in sustainable manufacturing practices.</p></p>
