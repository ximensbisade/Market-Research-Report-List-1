<p><p>The burglar alarm systems market is primarily categorized into two main types: wireless and wired alarm systems. Wireless alarm systems use radio signals to transmit alerts, offering flexibility in installation and expansion without extensive cabling. They are easy to set up and ideal for residential or commercial spaces where aesthetics or structural constraints matter. In contrast, wired alarm systems rely on physical cabling, providing robust connectivity and consistent power supply, often preferred for larger installations requiring reliable and uninterrupted performance.</p></p>
