<p><p>The artificial ceramic teeth market encompasses three primary types: all-ceramic, metal-ceramic, and others. All-ceramic teeth are made entirely from ceramic materials, offering aesthetic benefits and biocompatibility, making them ideal for anterior restorations. Metal-ceramic teeth combine a metal framework with ceramic layering, providing strength and durability, commonly used for posterior crowns. Other types include a variety of hybrid materials and advanced composites that blend properties for specific needs, catering to diverse patient preferences and clinical requirements.</p></p>
