<p><strong>The Aircraft Master Cylinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-type</li><li>Pull-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft master cylinders market is categorized into two main types: push-type and pull-type. Push-type master cylinders operate by pushing hydraulic fluid to activate the aircraft's control systems, commonly used in various landing gear and braking applications. Conversely, pull-type master cylinders function by pulling fluid to engage mechanisms, often seen in systems like thrust reversers. Each type serves specific aircraft needs, enabling efficient hydraulic operations critical for flight safety and performance.</p></p>
