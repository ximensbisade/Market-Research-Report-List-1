<p><p>Parenteral nutrition solutions are essential for patients unable to meet their nutritional needs through oral intake. The market is divided into two primary categories: Large Volume Parenteral (LVP) and Small Volume Parenteral (SVP). LVPs typically consist of nutrient-rich solutions including electrolytes and carbohydrates in volumes of 500 mL or more, used for long-term nutritional support. Conversely, SVPs contain smaller volumes (up to 500 mL) and often deliver concentrated nutrients, medications, or specialized formulations for short-term or specific nutritional therapy.</p></p>
