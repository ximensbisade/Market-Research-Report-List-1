<p><p>The Electronic Offender Monitoring Solutions market consists of two primary types: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS). RF technology uses short-range signals to track offenders within designated areas, providing a reliable means for monitoring compliance. In contrast, GPS technology offers real-time tracking over vast distances, enabling authorities to monitor an offender's location and movements accurately. Both solutions enhance public safety and support rehabilitation efforts by ensuring offenders adhere to legal restrictions and guidelines.</p></p>
