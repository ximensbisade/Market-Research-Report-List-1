<p><strong>The Automatic Motor Starters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Star Delta Starter</li><li>Direct-On-Line Starter</li><li>Rotor Resistance Starter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic motor starters market comprises several types, including Star Delta Starters, Direct-On-Line (DOL) Starters, and Rotor Resistance Starters, among others. Star Delta Starters reduce voltage during motor start-up, minimizing inrush current and mechanical stress. DOL Starters provide direct full voltage, suitable for small motors. Rotor Resistance Starters control the starting torque and current by adjusting the rotor resistance in slip ring motors. The "Others" category includes various advanced starters, tailored for specific motor applications and performance requirements.</p></p>
