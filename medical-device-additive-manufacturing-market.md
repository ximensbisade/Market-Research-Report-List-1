<p><strong>The Medical Device Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Sintering</li><li>Stereolithography</li><li>Electron Beam Melting</li><li>Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Device Additive Manufacturing market features several key technologies. Laser Sintering utilizes laser beams to fuse powdered materials, ideal for complex geometries. Stereolithography employs UV light to cure liquid resins layer by layer, suitable for intricate designs. Electron Beam Melting uses an electron beam to melt metal powders, providing high precision and density, particularly for implants. Extrusion involves pushing thermoplastic materials through a nozzle, allowing for rapid production of customized devices. Together, these technologies enhance innovation in medical device manufacturing.</p></p>
