<p><strong>The Spray Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Spray Dryer</li><li>Stream Spray Dryer</li><li>Centrifuge Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray dryer market consists of three main types: Pressure Spray Dryers, Stream Spray Dryers, and Centrifuge Spray Dryers. Pressure Spray Dryers use high pressure to atomize liquids into droplets, ensuring uniform drying. Stream Spray Dryers operate by directing a stream of air to evaporate moisture quickly, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Centrifuge Spray Dryers utilize centrifugal force to create fine droplets, offering efficient drying and reduced residence time, making them suitable for thermally sensitive products. Each type serves specific applications based on drying needs.</p></p>
