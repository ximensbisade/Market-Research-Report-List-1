<p><p>The multifamily modular construction market encompasses two main types: permanent modular and relocatable modular. Permanent modular construction involves factory-built units that are transported and assembled on-site, providing durable, long-lasting housing solutions. Conversely, relocatable modular units are designed for temporary use, allowing for easy relocation and repurposing as demand changes. Both types offer advantages such as faster construction times and reduced costs, making them appealing options in addressing housing shortages and evolving community needs.</p></p>
