<p><ul><li>CMOS Module</li><li>CCD Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular camera system market is primarily categorized into CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) modules. CMOS modules are favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities with digital circuits, making them ideal for mobile devices and compact cameras. In contrast, CCD modules are known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, often used in professional imaging applications such as medical imaging and astronomy. Both types cater to different market needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
