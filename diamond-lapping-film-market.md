<p><p>Diamond lapping films are categorized based on their backing types, which influence their applications and performance. PSA (Pressure Sensitive Adhesive) backing allows for easy attachment to various surfaces, making it ideal for quick changes during operations. Plain backing lacks adhesive, requiring mechanical fixation, and is often used for specialized tasks where precise control is vital. Other backing types may include magnetic or reusable options, catering to specific industrial needs. Each type offers distinct advantages for different lapping processes.</p></p>
