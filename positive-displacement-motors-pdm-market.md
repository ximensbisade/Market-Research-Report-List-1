The market can be segmented into rotor and stator types. Rotor-based PDMs utilize rotating elements to generate movement, while stator-based models rely on stationary components to achieve displacement. Both types are utilized across various applications, including oil and gas, automotive, and industrial processes, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in fluid movement.</p></p>
