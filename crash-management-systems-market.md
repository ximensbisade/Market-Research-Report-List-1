<p><ul><li>Front Crash Management</li><li>Rear Crash Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crash Management Systems (CMS) enhance vehicle safety by absorbing impact forces during collisions. The market is primarily divided into two types: Front Crash Management and Rear Crash Management. Front Crash Management focuses on optimizing structures to protect occupants during head-on collisions, often incorporating energy-absorbing materials and designs. Conversely, Rear Crash Management aims to mitigate injuries from rear-end collisions, utilizing features like energy-absorbing bumpers and reinforcement systems. Both systems are essential for improving overall vehicle safety and complying with regulatory standards.</p></p>
